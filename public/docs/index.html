The Complete Job Portal you need

InfyJobs is all in one Job Portal Laravel Scripts for all 3 types of users including Job Seekers, Employers and Portal Admin.

Get started

Features

Frontend Website

Get a beautiful getting started landing page for your website.

Admin Portal

All the information to manage at a glance for Portal Admin.

Candidates

Candidates can search for jobs and applied to the job to get hired.

Employers

Employers panel to manage all their job posting and hiring.

Dashboard

Effective & Beautiful dashboard for all 3 types of Users.

Post Jobs

Employers can post a job and manage job statuses.

Search Jobs

Candidates can search for a job based on their Skills & Category.

Apply to Jobs

Candidate can apply to jobs that are relevant to them.

Job Applications

Employers can manage job applications that they received.

Job Settings

Various Job settings which can be used while posting a job.

Skills

Manage different skills that are required to complete a job.

Blog

Blog posts and categories management and CMS.
